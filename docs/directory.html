LGBTQ+ Directory

Tulsa's Queer-Owned and Queer-Friendly Businesses

The most comprehensive list of LGBTQ+ owned and affirming businesses in Tulsa, Oklahoma. Whether you're looking for a great meal, a new tattoo, a place to worship, or your next favorite coffee shop, we've got you covered.

Know a business that should be listed? DM us on Instagram @tulsagays

LGBTQ+ Community Hubs

Oklahomans for Equality (OkEq)
Dennis R. Neill Equality Center, 621 E 4th St
Tulsa's flagship LGBTQ+ organization. Community programs, events, support groups, and the home base for queer life in Tulsa.
OkEq Business Alliance
Network of LGBTQ+ owned and allied businesses across Tulsa. Hosts mixers, networking events, and supports queer entrepreneurs.
Black Queer Tulsa
Community organization centering Black LGBTQ+ voices in Tulsa through events, gatherings, and advocacy.
Freedom Oklahoma
Statewide LGBTQ+ advocacy organization fighting for equality in Oklahoma policy and legislation.
Tulsa Pride
Organizers of the annual Tulsa Pride Festival, one of the largest pride celebrations in the region.
Tulsa PFLAG
Local chapter of Parents, Families, and Friends of Lesbians and Gays. Support, education, and advocacy for LGBTQ+ families.

MixCo

Health & Wellness

Diversity Family Health
LGBTQ+ affirming primary care clinic. Hormone therapy, PrEP, STI testing, and general healthcare in a welcoming environment.
H.O.P.E. Testing
Free and confidential HIV testing and prevention services. PrEP navigation, harm reduction, and community health outreach.
Tulsa CARES (TACO)
Comprehensive HIV/AIDS services including case management, housing assistance, and support programs for people living with HIV.
OkEq Health Programs
Health clinics and wellness programs run through the Equality Center. STI testing, mental health resources, and community health events.
Planned Parenthood of the Heartland
Reproductive and sexual health services. Gender-affirming care, hormone therapy, and inclusive healthcare for all.
Calm Waters
Grief and trauma counseling center offering LGBTQ+ inclusive therapy, support groups, and crisis resources.

Support Groups

Peer support group for transgender, nonbinary, and gender-questioning individuals. Safe space for sharing and connection.
SMART Recovery LGBTQ+
Evidence-based addiction recovery meetings with an LGBTQ+ focus. Self-management and recovery training in a supportive setting.
Lambda Unity AA
LGBTQ+ Alcoholics Anonymous meetings. A safe, affirming space for recovery and fellowship within the queer community.
PFLAG Tulsa
Monthly meetings for parents, families, and allies of LGBTQ+ people. Education, support, and advocacy for all families.
OkEq Youth Group
Dennis R. Neill Equality Center
Safe space for LGBTQ+ youth to connect, socialize, and find support. Regular meetings and activities at the Equality Center.
Trans Lifeline
National peer support hotline run by and for transgender people. Available for Tulsa community members at (877) 565-8860.
