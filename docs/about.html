About Tulsa Gays

What We Do

Tulsa Gays is your weekly LGBTQ+ event guide for Tulsa, Oklahoma. Every week, we aggregate and curate the best queer events happening around the city so you never miss a thing.

Whether it's community gatherings, drag shows, art exhibitions, happy hours, or nightlife events, we bring it all together in one place so you can plan your week and stay connected with the Tulsa LGBTQ+ community.

Our Sources

We aggregate events from across the Tulsa LGBTQ+ community, including:

  • Oklahomans for Equality (OKEQ) and the Dennis R. Neill Equality Center
  • Twisted Arts Tulsa
  • All Souls Unitarian Church
  • Church of the Restoration
  • Tulsa Eagle
  • Yellow Brick Road (YBR)
  • Majestic Night Club
  • Local community calendars and event listings
  • Homo Hotel Happy Hour

Know of an event source we're missing? Drop us a DM on Instagram and we'll add it to our radar.

Stay Connected

Follow us on Instagram at @tulsagays for event posts every Monday and Thursday. We post weekday events on Mondays and weekend events on Thursdays so you can plan ahead.

This website is updated weekly with the full event rundown, and you can always browse past weeks in our archive.
