THIS WEEK TULSA GAYS

March 30 — April 6, 2026

Nothing to do in Tulsa? Sounds like a straight person problem.

Featured Event of the Week
HOMO HOTEL HAPPY HOUR
Friday, April 3 · 6:00 – 8:00 PM
wxyz bar at Aloft Tulsa Downtown · 200 Civic Center
It's First Friday and that means HHHH is back, baby. This is the queerest happy hour in T-Town and honestly one of the best nights of the month. The wxyz bar at the Aloft is giving hotel lobby chic, the drink specials are solid, and the crowd is warm, welcoming, and genuinely fun to be around. Whether you're a regular or this is your very first time, just walk in and say hi to literally anyone. You'll feel at home in about five minutes flat.

Queer Women's Collective Happy Hour
Dennis R. Neill Equality Center · 621 E 4th St
First Wednesday of the month means QWC Happy Hour, and this one always delivers. It's a relaxed, no-pressure hang where queer women and their friends grab a drink and just enjoy each other's company. If you've been meaning to find your people in Tulsa, this is honestly one of the easiest rooms to walk into. Come solo or bring a friend, either way you'll leave with more connections than you came with. Runs 5:00 to 6:30.

Dennis R. Neill Equality Center · 621 E 4th St
