April 6 — April 12, 2026

Nothing to do in Tulsa? Sounds like a straight person problem.

Community Spotlight of the Week
COUNCIL OAK MEN'S CHORALE
Rehearsal Every Monday · 6:50 PM – 9:00 PM
All Souls Unitarian Church · 2952 S Peoria Ave
Tulsa's premier gay men's chorus is preparing something special. Their spring concert "Cabaret — Here Comes the Sun" is coming April 23. Monday rehearsals are open to new singers. If you can carry a tune and want a community that will change your life, show up at 6:50 PM. The chorus welcomes gay and gay-affirming men of all experience levels.

OSU Tulsa Queer Support Group
OSU Tulsa Campus · 700 N Greenwood Ave
Every Tuesday at 6 PM, OSU Tulsa hosts a free, open queer support group for adults. This is not a therapy session -- it's a gathering of real people navigating real stuff together. All Souls Unitarian Church Services
All Souls Unitarian · 2952 S Peoria Ave
Sunday services at All Souls at 10 AM and 11:15 AM. All Souls is the largest Unitarian Universalist congregation in the United States and has been a cornerstone of Tulsa's LGBTQ+ community for decades. The LGBTQ Justice Team is active, affirming, and welcoming. Whether you're spiritual, secular, or just looking for a community that actually walks its values, All Souls is worth showing up for. First-timers are welcomed warmly -- no performance required.
