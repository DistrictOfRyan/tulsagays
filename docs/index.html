HERE COMES THE SUN CABARET
Thursday, April 23 · 7:30 PM

The men of Council Oak Men's Chorale trade the risers for rhinestones. Solos, duets, and show-stopping harmony wrapped in golden, sun-drenched vibes. VIP 4-top tables with charcuterie boards are available, or grab general admission. Either way, bring applause and sunshine energy. This one fills up.
